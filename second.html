For the second assignment we were given a task to create a logo and use different Vector editing softwares. I have chosen three softwares: Inkscape, CorelDraw, Illustrator. Inkscape is the best choice because it is free software and everybody can use it.

I have experience in CorelDraw and a little bit in illustrator. I already had the knowledge of how vectors work so the hardest part was just finding where the tools were and getting used to the different editing options, finding and stumbling on to the new ways to edit shapes.
